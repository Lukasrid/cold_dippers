Nervous?


Thats that human survival instinct kicking in. The cold is something that most of us have been taught to avoid at all costs, but this has ended up costing us more harm than good. Research and personal expriences have shown that regular exposure to the cold can help quiet a stressful mind, improve your immune system, increase production of

brown adipose tissue (brown fat)

which burns up normal white fat cell to produce body heat. Like with any new venture this proccess takes time, dedication and a strong mindset. Through the Cold Dippers community we aim to provide support and motivation to help YOU reach your ultimate human potential!
