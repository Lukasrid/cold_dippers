About Us

Founded in 2018 the Cold Dippers is a small but ever growing community of people who are learning to embrace the cold in order to fortify their mind and body. The founders of the community were inspired by the ancient Tibetan Buddhist practice of Tummo which includes meditation and breathing practices to build an inner fire and over come the cold. With a similair and more modern approach Wim Hof, continued to draw out the curiosities of cold exposure with his own developed breath retention method nicknamed the Wim Hof Method. Following down this already paved path the founders soon experienced firsthand the power that comes with these practices. What started off with a simple word of mouth soon gained traction with friends and curious strangers which has led to this flourishing community of people who want to overcome the boundaries of their own comfort to achive feats which many people have deemed to be an impossible or dangerous task. Here at the Cold Dippers we put safety above all else to ensure that we can all reap the benefits of these practices. Anyone is welcome to come and participate, observe or ask questions at any of our events or meet-up. We have a strong policy to provide support instead of pressure for anyone who wishes to take the plunge. The experience tends to push people out of their comfort zones in the beginning, which is an important step for when you are trying to make a change to your body and mind. We will teach you methods that can be practiced on site or at home to help you prepare your body and mind to get comfortable with the immersion into cold water. It is a powerful experience that will mould you into something greater and more resilient, it will help start unlocking your ultimate human potential.
